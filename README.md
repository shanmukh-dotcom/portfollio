# 🌍 The World of Shanmukh

> *A portfolio where you don't scroll—you explore.*

🔗 **[Experience the Live Portfolio Here!](https://shanmukh-portfolio.pages.dev)**

## 📖 Overview

**The World of Shanmukh** is an immersive, medieval fantasy-inspired interactive portfolio that transforms a traditional resume into a story-driven adventure.

Instead of navigating through web pages, visitors enter a handcrafted fantasy village where every character represents a different chapter of my journey. By exploring the world, interacting with NPCs, and uncovering magical artifacts, visitors gradually discover my projects, skills, education, achievements, ambitions, and contact information.

The goal is to make a portfolio memorable—not by adding more information, but by changing **how** that information is experienced.

---

# ✨ The Experience

The journey begins as a traveler steps through a magical portal into a peaceful fantasy village.

The player is free to explore and meet six unique inhabitants.

Each character represents a different aspect of my professional journey.

Instead of menus and sections, every interaction unlocks a unique visual experience.

# 🎨 Design Philosophy

The project is built around one simple idea:

> Information is remembered better when it is discovered.

Rather than overwhelming visitors with a resume or navigation menu, the portfolio encourages exploration and curiosity.

Every section has its own visual identity and interaction style.

# 🎯 Why This Project?

Recruiters and visitors often spend only a short time reviewing a portfolio.

This project aims to transform that experience into something memorable by replacing traditional scrolling with exploration, storytelling, and interaction.

Rather than simply presenting information, **The World of Shanmukh** invites visitors to experience the journey behind it.
